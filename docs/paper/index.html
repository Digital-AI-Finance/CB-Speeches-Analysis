Central Bank Communication and Macroeconomic Conditions

Taibi & Osterrieder (2025) - University of Zurich

Abstract: Analysis of Federal Reserve speech sentiment and macroeconomic conditions (1996-2025). Using PCA to construct macro indices and PELT for structural breaks, we document near-zero correlation (r = 0.005) between sentiment and economic fundamentals.
